Instructions

How to play:

  • Reach 20 points within the time limit to pass the level!
  • Complete each level by avoiding the enemy characters
  • Kill flying enemies by jumping on them
  • Killing an enemy grants you bonus score
  • Avoid things on the ground by jumping over them

Controls:

  • Press 'w' to jump
  • Press 'a' to move left
  • Press 'd' to move right
  • Press 's' to sit
  • Previous Level
  • Next Level
